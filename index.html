Since 2023, I have been developing various video games in both Unreal Engine and Unity, some of which have been launched on platforms such as Google Play Store and Itch.io, enhancing my skills as a Game Designer and team player. Nowadays, I am an entrepreneur at Tachido Games as a Co-Founder and Game Designer, gaining more professional experience.
